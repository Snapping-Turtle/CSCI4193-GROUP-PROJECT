Our Goal

We have a clear goal

Our goal is to ensure easy access to information about vaccines that is actually relevant to the user

We want everyone to be able to quickly find the nearest location to recieve their vaccinations. As well as provide links to other reputable sources on vaccines.

Our Plan

Start Focused

We found through surveys that people would use our app if they did not have to pay for it.

We plan to target universitys to remove the immediate cost from the user. We plan to target schools with a high number of exchange students.
